Education plays an important role in shaping a person’s future. Schools and universities provide knowledge, but self-learning is equally valuable in today’s fast-changing world. Online platforms have made it possible for students to learn almost any subject from anywhere with an internet connection.

Developing problem-solving skills is often more important than memorizing facts. Students who practice regularly and work on real-world projects usually gain a deeper understanding of concepts. Collaboration, communication, and critical thinking are essential skills in both academic and professional environments.
